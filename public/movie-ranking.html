The Best Movies According to Yazan

My Top 3 movies of last-year.

Black Adam

the spiky and majestic "Black Adam" is one of the best DC superhero films to date

The Lion King

a childhood-shaping, Oscar-winning blockbuster, the second-highest grossing feature film of its calendar year, one of the last great hand-drawn Disney animated features

Mowgli Legend Of The Jungle

somewhere between a film that's for kids and yet, in its persistent cruelty and bleakness, definitely not for kids.
The whole movie feels oddly stranded and dramatically inert, despite the obvious passion that went into making it.
